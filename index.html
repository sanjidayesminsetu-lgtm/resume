Who I Am

Research, Nature & Purpose

I am a Zoology graduate and current Master's student in Fisheries at National University, Bangladesh. My academic journey has been rooted in a deep curiosity about living systems — from freshwater ecosystems to the health of adolescent communities.

As a Field Research Assistant with BRAC JPGSPH, I work on the AMEHC Project, gathering and safeguarding critical data on adolescent menstrual health. I believe rigorous science, paired with empathy, drives real-world change.

Outside of research, I co-founded RootRise Charity, channelling community resources toward meaningful social impact.
